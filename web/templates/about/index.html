About CV Extractor

Revolutionizing resume processing with artificial intelligence

Our Mission

To democratize access to advanced AI-powered resume processing, making it easy for businesses and individuals to extract structured data from CVs with unprecedented accuracy.

We believe that hiring processes should be efficient, fair, and data-driven. Our platform eliminates the tedious manual work of parsing resumes, allowing HR professionals and recruiters to focus on what matters most - finding the right talent.

Our Technology

Built on cutting-edge AI and machine learning technologies

Large Language Models

Powered by state-of-the-art LLMs including Llama 3, Mistral, and Microsoft Phi for accurate text understanding and extraction.

OCR Technology

Advanced optical character recognition using Google's Gemini AI to handle both text-based and scanned PDF documents.

Security & Privacy

Enterprise-grade security with automatic document deletion and no permanent data storage to protect user privacy.
